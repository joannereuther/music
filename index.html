History of Music

Different forms of music has been around for centries. Used in many different ways over time, music has been a resource everyone has used at least once in their life. The Middle Ages had recorded two different types of music, the monophinic and the polphonic. These forms of music were mainly chanting and forms of church music with no intrumental accompaniment. Druing the Renaissance Era, instruments became more popular and people started to sing together in larger groups. During the Classical period music was characterized by simpler melodies and quiter music. It was also at this time where the accessablity of music was increased thus allowing more people to listen and use the resource. The Romantic Music period between 1800 and 1900 music was celebrated and the introduction of wind instruments changed the course of music production. Melodies became fuller and more dramatic through the use of saxophone and the flute. Now in the 20th and 21st Century, music is produced through the use of techknowledgy where the different forms and processes of music is shared.
